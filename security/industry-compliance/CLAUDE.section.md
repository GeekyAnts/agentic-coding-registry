## Security: Industry Compliance (as applicable)

Regulatory and industry compliance regimes — apply the ones that cover your data,
sector, and regions.

- **[GDPR](https://gdpr.eu):** EU personal-data protection.
- **[PCI DSS](https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org):** payment-card data.
- **[HIPAA](https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa):** US protected health information.
- **[FedRAMP](https://www.fedramp.gov):** US federal cloud authorization.
- **[SOC 2](https://www.aicpa-cima.com/topic/audit-assurance/audit-and-assurance-greater-than-soc-2):**
  trust-services criteria (security, availability, confidentiality, …).
- **[India DPDP Act](https://www.meity.gov.in/data-protection-framework):** Digital
  Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

**Apply here:**
- Identify which regimes apply (data types, sector, user regions) and treat their
  requirements as hard constraints.
- Minimize and classify personal / sensitive data; encrypt it at rest and in transit;
  enforce least-privilege access + audit logging.
- Support data-subject rights (access / erasure / portability) and breach-notification
  timelines where required.
- Keep processing records and evidence for audits; don't store regulated data you don't
  need.
