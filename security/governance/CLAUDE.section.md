## Security: Enterprise Governance

Risk-management and information-security governance frameworks.

- **[ISO/IEC 27001](https://www.iso.org/standard/27001):** Information Security Management
  System (ISMS) — risk-based controls, documented policies, continual improvement.
- **[NIST CSF 2.0](https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework):** Govern, Identify, Protect,
  Detect, Respond, Recover.
- **[ISO 31000](https://www.iso.org/standard/65694.html):** enterprise risk-management
  principles and process.

**Apply here:**
- Keep security policies, data classification, and access reviews documented and current.
- Maintain an asset / data inventory and a risk register; map controls to the framework
  you report against.
- Log and retain evidence (access, changes, incidents) to support audits.
- Maintain and periodically test an incident-response plan.
